Alert Categories

Three important environmental alerts used in this project.

Critical

High Pollution Alert

Condition: AQI > 200

Alert Message: High Pollution Alert: The air quality in this state has crossed the safe limit. Immediate pollution control measures such as reducing vehicle emissions and industrial pollution are recommended to protect environmental and public health.

Purpose: Helps authorities quickly identify highly polluted states and take action.

Forest Risk

Forest Improvement Alert

Condition: Forest Cover < 15%

Alert Message: Forest Improvement Alert: The forest cover in this state is below the recommended level. Increasing tree plantation and developing green zones are necessary to improve environmental balance and reduce pollution impact.

Purpose: Encourages increasing forest cover to reduce pollution and improve environmental sustainability.

Combined Risk

Environmental Risk Alert

Condition: AQI high and Forest Cover low

Alert Message: Environmental Risk Alert: This state has been identified with high pollution levels and low forest cover. Immediate environmental action, including plantation programs and emission control measures, is strongly recommended to reduce ecological risk.

Purpose: Identifies combined environmental risks and supports decision-making for environmental improvement.
